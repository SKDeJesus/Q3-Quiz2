"Up" is an animated adventure film that follows the story of Carl Fredricksen, an elderly widower who fulfills his dream of visiting South America by tying thousands of balloons to his house and unintentionally bringing along a young boy named Russell. Cast: Pete Docter, Jordan Nagai, Bob Peterson Director: Pete Docter Duration: 1h 36m Anonymous: One hundred years from now, film scholars will study the 15 wordless minutes near the beginning of this movie as one of the finest examples of storytelling.
